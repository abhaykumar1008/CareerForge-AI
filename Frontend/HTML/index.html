Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about CareerForge AI.

❓ What is CareerForge AI?

CareerForge AI is an AI-powered placement preparation platform that helps students improve their resumes, identify skill gaps, prepare for interviews, and track their placement progress.

❓ Is CareerForge AI free to use?

Yes. The basic features are available for free. Premium features and advanced AI tools may be introduced in future versions.

❓ Which companies are supported?

CareerForge AI helps students prepare for companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, and many more.

❓ How does Resume Analysis work?

Upload your resume and the system evaluates its ATS score, identifies missing skills, and provides suggestions to improve your resume.

❓ Can I track my placement progress?

Yes. Your dashboard displays your learning progress, placement readiness score, completed roadmaps, certificates, and job applications.

❓ Can I access CareerForge AI on mobile?

Yes. CareerForge AI is designed to be fully responsive, allowing you to access it from desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
